For You

I didn’t make this to convince you.
Just to show you clearly who I am.

Where I went wrong

I got angry over small things instead of staying calm.
I reacted instead of trying to understand you.
I didn’t always listen fully before responding.
Sometimes I made things about me when they weren’t.
And I know my actions didn’t always match my words.

What I’m changing

I’m learning to understand you more instead of reacting.
I pause when I feel angry instead of responding instantly.
I don’t expect attention or replies when you’re busy.
I’m focusing on my routine and building my own stability.
I’m working on being someone whose actions match his words.
And I know that if anything has to work in the future, it will come from consistency, not promises.

What I understand now

I understand that trust isn’t built by saying things once—it’s built over time through actions.

I also understand that if I ever want a place in your life, it’s not just about you—it’s about being someone who deserves that place completely.

Not just in your eyes, but also in front of Harish uncle and Preeti aunty.

And that only comes from real change, consistency, and maturity—not words.

Just one thing I want to ask you

Not for pressure. Just for honesty.

Do you feel there is still something here worth giving a chance to?

No matter what you choose, this is who I’m becoming.

— Vansh

Thank you

That’s enough for me.
I’ll prove the rest with time.

It’s okay

Take your time.
I’ll still work on myself.
